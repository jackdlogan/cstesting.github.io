Process for internship

  • Internship takes place during the 6th semester of the bachelor degree. The internship consist of a project in an enterprise. The formal duration of the internship should be at least 14 weeks.
  • To be allowed to go in the internship, students must have completed with success all his undergraduate courses, i.e., students must have accumulated 129 credits.
  • Students must submit an application to the enterprise where they want to do internship
  • The internship must be a project in computer science
  • Student should ask for a ”certificate” from the enterprise where they do their internship that specify in some way what they will do there, for how long (14 weeks)
  • Internship is graded by a professor in the CS dept. The evaluation consists of a report and a presentation (seminar). The grade is pass or fail.

Process for bachelor thesis

  • The bachelor thesis takes place during the 6th semester of the bachelor degree. Its starts after the internship is completed, and consist of a research/application project selected by the student and his adviser. The formal duration of the internship is 9 weeks.
  • The bachelor thesis is supervised by a lecturer at VGU and co-supervise by a second lecturer
  • Topic could be related to their work during the internship.
  • Students must register for the thesis like for any other examination
  • The thesis is first evaluated by the two supervisors, if pass, then can go for the defense
  • Defense can be with or without a presentation. Can be open to the public

Repeatability of examination performances

(1) Failed module examination performances or failed partial module examination performances for the modules M1 to M27 can be repeated twice.
(2) The module M28 Bachelor’s thesis with colloquium may only be repeated once.
(3) Passed examination performances and partial module examination performances cannot be repeated.
